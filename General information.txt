
<h3 style="margin-top:0px;">General Information</h3>
<p>
We are excited to announce the Beijing Logic Meeting 2023, a premier academic conference that will bring together leading researchers and practitioners in the field of logic from around the world. 
<br>The conference will be held in <strong>Beijing, China</strong>, from <strong>24-28 July 2023</strong>, 
will feature talks in Model Theory, Set Theory and Computability.



<h3>Conference Schedule </h3>
<p>
   <p><strong>Conference dates: 24-28 July 2023.</strong></p>
   <p><strong>Schedule: </strong>There will be five speakers reporting every day from Monday to Thursday, and <strong>Friday is free.</strong></p>
   <p><strong>Venue: Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences.</strong></p>
