General Information

We are excited to announce the Beijing Logic Meeting 2023, a premier academic conference that will bring together leading researchers and practitioners in the field of logic from around the world.
The conference will be held in Beijing, China, from 24-28 July 2023, and will feature three workshops: Model Theory, Set Theory and Computability with talks, panels, and discussions on the latest developments in logic.

Conference Schedule

Conference dates: 24-28 July 2023.

Schedule: There will be five speakers reporting every day from Monday to Thursday, and Friday is free.

Venue: Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Lunch and Dinner: Banquet style.

We will reimburse the travel expenses from overseas as well as the cost of lunch and dinner for everyone.

Featured Speakers

  • Model theory
  • Computability
